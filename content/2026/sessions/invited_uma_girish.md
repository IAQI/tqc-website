## Bio

Uma Girish is a postdoctoral research scientist in computer science at Columbia University, hosted by Henry Yuen, and will join the University of Toronto as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2026. She received her PhD from Princeton University in 2024 under the supervision of Ran Raz, following degrees at the Chennai Mathematical Institute. Her research explores the interplay between quantum computing, complexity theory, and the analysis of Boolean functions, including quantum query algorithms, communication complexity, and quantum information theory.
