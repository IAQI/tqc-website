## Bio

Sevag Gharibian is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Paderborn University, Germany, where he is affiliated with the Institute for Photonic Quantum Systems (PhoQS). His research spans algorithms, complexity, and quantum computation, with a focus on quantum complexity theory, quantum proof systems and unentangled provers, Hamiltonian complexity, and the computational power of quantum systems.
