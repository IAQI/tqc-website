The TQC 2026 Local Organizing Committee has reserved a portion of the conference budget to provide financial support for
students attending the conference.

## How to Apply

To apply for student support, please send an email to [2026@tqc-conference.org](mailto:2026@tqc-conference.org) no later than **Sunday, May 31, 2026** (23:59,
AoE timezone) with all of the following information:

* Brief statement of financial need (maximum 100 words).
* Current studies program, level, and institution.
* A short explanation (maximum 100 words) of how your work relates to TQC.
* Optional statement of gender identity and/or membership in an underrepresented group, if applicable.
* Description of other funding sources available to you, or a specific plan to secure additional funding.
* Letter from your supervisor, advisor, or another referee explicitly stating:
  * the amount of financial support available for your participation in TQC, or
  * that no such support is available.
* Email subject line (must be exact): TQC 2026 Student Support – Name(s) SURNAME(S)

## Selection Criteria
Preference will be given to student applicants in one or more of the following categories:
* Authors or co-authors of papers accepted at TQC 2026 who would otherwise be unable to attend due to financial constraints.
* Participants from underrepresented groups.

## Selection Committee
Applications will be reviewed by a committee composed of:
* **Honghao Fu**, Concordia University
* **Stefanos Kourtis**, Université de Sherbrooke
* **Ayana Sarkar**, Université de Sherbrooke

## Support Package
* **Students based in Canada and USA**: up to **CAD $875**
  (includes conference registration fee)
* **Students based outside of Canada and USA**: up to **CAD $1,300**
  (includes conference registration fee)

## Notification
Applicants will be informed of funding decisions by **June 14, 2026**. The exact award amount will be detailed in the
individual award notification letter. To remain eligible for the support package, recipients must complete their
conference registration **no later than June 28, 2026**.
