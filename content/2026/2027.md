## Proposals to host TQC 2027
This will be handled by a 2-step process:
1. If you are interested, please send a letter of intent to
{{< button-link label="tqc-steer@googlegroups.com" url="mailto:tqc-steer@googlegroups.com" icon="email">}} with the title "letter of intent to host TQC 2027" by <strong> December 19, 2025 </strong>, including:
- Name and affiliation of main organizer
- Possible conference dates
- Location and size of intended conference venue
2. The steering committee will request the top contenders, by January 9, 2026, to submit a full proposal, to be submitted by February 6, 2026.
